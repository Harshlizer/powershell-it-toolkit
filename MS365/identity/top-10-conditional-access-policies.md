# Top 10 Conditional Access Policies for Production

## Baseline

1. Require MFA for all users
2. Block legacy authentication
3. Block access outside trusted countries or regions
4. Block access outside trusted networks
5. Require compliant devices for Microsoft 365 access
6. Require MFA for administrators
7. Require phishing-resistant MFA for administrators
8. Block or remediate high user risk
9. Require MFA on medium and high sign-in risk
10. Require approved client apps or app protection on mobile

## Rollout Rule

Always start in `Report-only`, validate impact, and exclude emergency accounts deliberately.
