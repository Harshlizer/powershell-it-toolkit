# Prevent Microsoft Teams Sprawl

## Practical Controls

1. Restrict team creation to approved users
2. Add approval workflows for new team requests
3. Enforce naming policies
4. Limit channel creation to owners where appropriate
5. Apply group expiration policies
6. Identify and fix ownerless teams
7. Archive inactive teams
8. Audit teams, channels, members, and owners
9. Monitor recent team and channel creation through audit logs
10. Educate users on when to create a new team versus reuse an existing one
